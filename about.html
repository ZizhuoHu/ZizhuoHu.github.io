About Me

I am a level designer studying at SMU Guildhall, with a focus on FPS spaces, encounter pacing, and player-readable layouts. My work often explores how geometry, sightlines, verticality, and objective placement shape player behavior.

I am especially interested in multiplayer and co-op shooter maps, including TDM, CTF, Zombies-style spaces, and scripted single-player combat encounters.
