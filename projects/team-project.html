Overview

Neko Moto is a team-developed 2D action platformer. I worked on level design with an emphasis on clear onboarding, readable routes, and spaces that support the game's movement and combat systems.

ConceptBlockoutIterationPolish

Design Goals

My goal was to introduce mechanics through a safe-to-dangerous learning curve, build readable platforming challenges, and create level sections that fit the team's combat, movement, and pacing goals.
